BIODATA

Hello peeps! My name is Nur Balqis Habibi binti Yusdi and I was born in Terengganu. I am in my last year of a Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Kelantan's Machang branch. My birthdate is on July 6, 2002, and I will be 21 years old in the next few months. I am the oldest of four siblings. My elementary school was Sekolah Kebangsaan Sultan Sulaiman II, and my secondary school was Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Sultan Sulaiman.

HOBBY

To be honest, it is hard for me to focus on one particular hobby since I am the type of person that gets bored quickly with everything I do. However, whenever I have personal time, I enjoy reading English literature and working out. Reading is my pleasure since it allows me to improve my grammatical skills. Not only that, but I enjoy writing short poems too because it is one of the ways I express myself.

So here is one of the short poem that I have written...

Life is unfair,
life is a part of hell;
it hurts us in many ways
in terms of emotions,
physicals and mental,
they are all broken.

Living in the dark,
tears slipping out
blood flowing down
motions turning dreary;
without realising,
eyes are slowly closing.

Life is ruthless,
life is all about suffering,
if you can't get through this
you will be dying.

AMBITION

I originally aspired to be an English teacher, but fate had other plans for me. Yet, I am not giving up on my dream and have modified my aim to become a lecturer in the future. The path I shall choose may be challenging, but I know myself better now. I enjoy sharing my knowledge with others, and it brings me joy when someone understands what I have taught them.
