F A M I L Y

I live separately from my siblings. My grandmother raised me from the time I was born till today, and I've also been raised by my second family since I was 11 years old.
Without them, I would not have been as successful as I am now. This year marks the 60th birthday of my grandma, and my adoptive father is the same age as my grandmother
while my mother is in her fifties. My adoptive father is a retired secondary school teacher, and my mother works as a clerk. I also have three adoptive siblings, one brother,
and two sisters. My older brother and sister are both married, while my younger sister is currently studying at UiTM Pahang's Jengka branch.
